Chapter 1 Movie Industry Analysis

Data source: IMDB Dataset Movie Lens

1.0.1 Description:

What’s important to rating? Director or Actor? In the IMDB dataset, join the “crew”, “principals” and “ratings” datasets, to get crew.directors, principals.actors, and ratings.averageRatings for the same title.

  1. How does age affect actor popularity? A. Get actors from the principals dataset (principals.category == “Actor”), their age from the basics dataset (basic.age = todayYear() - basics.birthYear), and averageRatings from the ratings dataset.

Do directors prefer to work with particular actors? Get directors from the crew.directors and actors from principals.category.

What actors tend to work with which actors? Use the principals to get the category==”Actor” column.

What percentage of actors also work as producers, writers, etc.? Is it always the top-performing actors who become producers and directors? Using the name.basics extract the professions using primaryProfessions.

Actors’ preference towards genre? And is there preference working out for them? To get the genres use title.basics and for actors use title.principals.category.

How common is it for actors to be pigeonholed in a role or genre, which they started in? Use actors from title.principals.category, genre from title.basics and ratings from title.ratings.averageRating.
